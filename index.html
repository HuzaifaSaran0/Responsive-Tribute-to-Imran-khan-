Here's a time line of Imran Khan's life:

  • 1952 "- Born in Lahore, Pakistan."
  • 1971 - Emerges as a cricketing icon, leading Pakistan to its first-ever World Cup victory in 1992.
  • 1996 - Transitions from cricket to politics, founding the Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) party with a vision for a corruption-free and equitable Pakistan.
  • 1994 - Establishes Shaukat Khanum Memorial Cancer Hospital & Research Centre in Lahore, realizing a dream to provide state-of-the-art cancer treatment to all, regardless of their financial means.
  • 2018 - Achieves a historic victory in the general elections, becoming the 22nd Prime Minister of Pakistan.
  • 2019 -Launches ambitious initiatives such as the Ehsaas Program for poverty alleviation and the Kamyab Jawan Program for youth development.
  • 2020 - Navigates the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic, implementing measures to safeguard public health and the economy.
  • 2021 - Advocates for peace and stability in the region, fostering diplomatic relations amid regional tensions.
  • 2023 - In jail, still fighting for his Nation's Interest.
